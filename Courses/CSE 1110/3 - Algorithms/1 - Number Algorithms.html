Number Algorithms

Algorithms are techniques. They are ways to accomplish a certain task. They are used in multiple locations to solve different goals. Writing algorithms is similar to teaching the computer how to do certain things.

Algorithms can vary in complexity, technique and form. Ultimately, the important part of algorithms is their use. If an algorithms doesn't work, it doesn't have value. This includes things called edge cases. Edge cases are theoretical situations in which the input to an algorithm is at the very edge of the realm of possibility. Algorithms must account for these.
